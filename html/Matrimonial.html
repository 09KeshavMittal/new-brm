MATRIMONIAL

India is home to several cultures and traditions with each having its own set of acceptable practices when it comes to marriage, divorce and other family disputes. Sarvaya Legal respects and understands the laws of every such religion and provides ethical solutions and legal assistance pertaining to all laws of marriage and divorce.

The Firm also assists and represents it clients before the various District Court, High Courts and the Supreme Court in matters related to family disputes, maintenance, child adoption and custody, domestic violence, transfer petitions and bail petitions and also assists in mediation and conciliation in such matters.
