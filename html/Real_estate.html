REAL ESTATE

Property development is a prominent market and one of the largest employers in the nation. In recent years, the enactment of the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016, as amended from time to time, has changed the way in which builders and developers had been functioning over the years, by putting accountability on their shoulders, thereby making real estate purchases by buyers transparent. Further, new revenue models have made real estate players seek legal advice much more frequently than before.

Professionals at Sarvaya Legal have a rich experience in the real estate sector and provide advice on compliance of RERA and rules, delay in possession, completion of projects and drafting and complaints and appeals before the RERA Tribunal and RERA Appellate, the District and State Consumer Forums and also in in the National Consumer Dispute Redressal Forum (NCDRC) in addition to the High Court and Supreme Court. The Firm also represents real estate developers and buyers before the Real Estate Regulatory Authority in various proceedings under RERA.

The Firm also assists its clients in documentation and registration of the same as well as registration of real estate projects with the Authority.
