ARBITRATION

Sarvaya Legal represents its clients in arbitration matters before Arbitral Tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India with its strategic approach.

With a deep and clear understanding of the client's goals, the Firm provides consultancy pertaining to arbitration disputes and on contentious issues arising out of general civil disputes, builder buyer agreements, indirect tax issues and has expertise in drafting arbitration petitions including Special Leave Petitions (SLPs) and appeals before the Supreme Court of India.

The Firm's expertise in arbitration matters includes:

  • Drafting and filing of arbitration petitions
  • Drafting and filing of SLPs and appeals before the Supreme Court of India
  • Representation before Arbitral Tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India
  • Consultancy on contentious issues arising out of general civil disputes, builder buyer agreements, indirect tax issues
